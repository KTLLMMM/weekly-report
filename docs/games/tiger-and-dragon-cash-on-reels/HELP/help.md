## 01 — TIGER BONUS

3 [Tiger symbol] in any position on the reels triggers the Tiger Bonus.
If 1 or 2 [Tiger symbol] appear in any position on the reels, the Tiger Bonus may be randomly awarded.
Win 3 bonus spins.
Touch START to start the bonus.
Each unlocked symbol position is a separate reel during the bonus. Only [Coin symbol] symbols are available during the bonus.
After each bonus spin, [Coin symbol] appearing on one or more unlocked reels resets the number of bonus spins remaining to 3. All [Coin symbol] symbols lock for the remainder of the bonus. If all reels lock, any remaining bonus spins are not played.
When 0 bonus spins remain or all reels lock, each [Coin symbol] symbol appearing in a cluster of 2 or more horizontally adjacent reels reveals a value of 1 to 99 or [Up arrow].
Only 1 double digit value or 1 [Up arrow] may appear in a cluster of 5 [Coin symbol] symbols. Up to 3 [Up arrow] or 2 double digit values may appear in a cluster of 4 [Coin symbol] symbols. [Up arrow] cannot appear in the leftmost position of a cluster.
Each [Up arrow] appearing in a cluster transforms into 0 and increases the leftmost value in the cluster by a value of 1 to 90.
After all transformations are complete, win the credit value displayed in each cluster, read as a single value from left to right.
The total bonus win is awarded and the Tiger Bonus ends.
Different reels are used during the Tiger Bonus. Reels are determined at the start of each bonus spin and depend on the bet option.

## 03 — TIGER AND DRAGON BONUS（上）

Any combination of 5 [Tiger symbol] and [Dragon symbol] in any position on the reels triggers the Tiger and Dragon Bonus.
If 1 or 2 [Tiger symbol] and 1 or 2 [Dragon symbol] appear in any position on the reels, the Tiger and Dragon Bonus may be randomly awarded.
Win 3 bonus spins.
Touch START to start the bonus.
At the start of the bonus, rows 1 to 3 are unlocked.
Each unlocked symbol position is a separate reel during the bonus. Only [Coin symbol] symbols are available during the bonus.
Collect the number of required [Coin symbol] symbols displayed on a locked row to unlock that row:
- a total of 6 [Coin symbol] in unlocked rows unlocks row 4
- a total of 9 [Coin symbol] in unlocked rows unlocks row 5
- a total of 14 [Coin symbol] in unlocked rows unlocks row 6
- a total of 19 [Coin symbol] in unlocked rows unlocks row 7
- a total of 25 [Coin symbol] in unlocked rows unlocks row 8

If [Coin symbol] appears on one or more unlocked reels in unlocked rows after a bonus spin, the number of bonus spins remaining resets to 3, the number of [Coin symbol] symbols appearing on unlocked reels in unlocked rows are collected in each [lock icon] meter, and all symbol positions containing [Coin symbol] in all rows lock for the remainder of the bonus.

## 07 — RULES

Reels are determined at the start of each base game and depend on the bet option.
MultiWay game is played at 243 ways.
MultiWay wins contain one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel.
The same symbol in a different position on the same reel pays that MultiWay win again.
Only the highest win is paid for each MultiWay symbol combination.
Wins on different played ways are added.
Increasing the bet increases the chance of winning the Jackpot.
Increasing the bet increases the chance of 4 or 5 [Coin symbol] symbols appearing in a cluster.
An increased number of coins in the Tiger Bonus and Dragon Bonus chests and an increased number of rotating [Coin symbol] on the large gong above the reels has no effect on game play.
Wins are shown in credits unless marked as currency.
Wins subject to verification.
Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
