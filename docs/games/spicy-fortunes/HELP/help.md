規則：
- All wins begin with leftmost reel and pay left to right on adjacent reels **except for [辣椒 chili／gems] prizes**。
- **[WILD]（戴帽辣椒）substitutes for all symbols except [粉鑽／藍鑽／綠鑽／彩鑽 gems]。**
- WILD appears on **reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 only**。
- When 2 or 3 WILD on the same reel are part of line wins, they appear as [雙/三 疊戴帽辣椒] respectively（堆疊 WILD）。
- All wins shown with the bet multiplier at 1.

## help-02 — JACKPOTS（彩金）
- **Grand and Major are progressives.**（累積）
- **Minor and Mini are fixed prizes.**（固定）
- Grand and Major are **not** multiplied by the bet multiplier. Minor and Mini **are** multiplied by the bet multiplier as shown on the screen.
- Once the Jackpot ceiling amount has been reached, further contributions are added to subsequent Jackpot progressives.
- Jackpot meter amount is reset each time it has been paid, before any other Jackpot is paid.
- An increase in wager increases the odds of winning progressives proportionally.
- Odds of Minor and Mini change with the bet multiplier and denomination.
- ※ 注意：只有 Grand／Major 累積、Minor／Mini 固定；無 Maxi 段。

## help-05 — SPICY FORTUNES FEATURES（特色觸發）
- Any one or more [鑽] in a spin may trigger **Extra Spins** feature.
- Any one or more [鑽] in a spin may trigger **Extra Pays** feature.
- Any one or more [鑽] in a spin may trigger **Extra Rows** feature.
- If more than one feature is triggered in a spin, they are combined and awarded.
- At the start of any feature, any [鑽] in the triggering spin will appear as [X]，which then converts to [辣椒]。
- Features are played on **3×5 reel matrix**. Bonus reels are in play. **3 bonus spins are awarded initially.**
- Any position containing [鑽/辣椒] is considered filled. On each bonus spin, any position containing [鑽/辣椒] is held and all other positions spin individually.

## help-06 — SPICY FORTUNES FEATURES（續）
- Every time one or more additional [鑽/辣椒] lands during a bonus spin, the number of bonus spins remaining is **reset to 3**。
- Each [鑽] upgrades any one [X] to [Y]，or any one [Y] to [Z] randomly.
- If no upgrade possible, each [鑽] will add one [辣椒] to the position it lands on.
- If all positions are filled during any Spicy Fortunes feature, 3 upgrades are awarded.（各列升級規則見下）
- The feature ends when remaining bonus spins are completed or all positions are filled and all upgrades are done. 結束時各 [辣椒] 顯示的獎項派發。The bet multiplier is the same as the game that triggered the feature.

## help-07 — EXTRA SPINS ／ EXTRA PAYS
**EXTRA SPINS**：4 bonus spins are awarded instead of 3 when Extra Spins is activated. Every time one or more additional [鑽/辣椒] lands during a bonus spin, the number of bonus spins remaining is **reset to 4**. If all positions are filled, **4 upgrades** are awarded instead of 3.
**EXTRA PAYS**：At the start of any feature with Extra Pays active, any [鑽] in the triggering spin will appear as [X]，which converts to [辣椒]。During the feature, [辣椒] may appear on any position instead of [X]。Each [鑽] upgrades any one [X] to [Y] or any one [Y] to [Z] randomly.

## help-08 — EXTRA PAYS（續）／ EXTRA ROWS
**EXTRA PAYS（續）**：If no upgrade possible, each [鑽] will add one [辣椒] to the position it lands on. If all positions are filled, [辣椒] may be upgraded as per the upgrade rules defined previously.
**EXTRA ROWS**：3 additional rows are added and feature is played on **6×5 reel matrix** when Extra rows is active.

## help-09 — WHEEL FEATURE（轉盤特色 + 轉盤獎表）
- After any single or combined Spicy Fortunes feature triggers during bought games, **one spin of WHEEL is awarded**. Touch SPIN or press PLAY to spin the wheel. The prize awarded is determined by the section of the wheel that the [指針] points to when the wheel stops. Based on the feature triggered, wheel may award [jackpots]、additional Spicy Fortune features 或 **Super Spicy wheel**。
- 轉盤獎表（FEATURE TRIGGER → WHEEL PRIZES）：依觸發組合（Extra Pays／Extra Rows／Extra Spins 及其組合）給 Grand/Major/Minor/Mini、額外 Extra 特色、或 Super Spicy Wheel（詳見圖，小字數值待核對）。

## help-10 — WHEEL FEATURE（續）
- Each [鑽] will add [辣椒] to an empty position at the start of Spicy Fortune feature.（列出各鑽對應）
- Odds of Spicy Fortune wheel prizes change with the bet multiplier and denomination.

## help-11 — SUPER SPICY WHEEL（超級轉盤 + 獎表）
- **One spin of Super Spicy wheel can be won from Wheel feature.** Touch SPIN or press PLAY to spin the wheel.
- The prize awarded is determined by the section of the wheel that the [指針] points to when the wheel stops. Super Spicy Wheel may award [jackpots]、upgrade current features to Super features、或 award additional Super features。
- 獎表（FEATURE TRIGGER → WHEEL PRIZES）：Grand/Major + Super Extra Pays/Rows/Spins 各種組合（詳見圖，數值待核對）。

## help-12 — SUPER FEATURES（超級特色）
- Depending upon initial Spicy Fortunes feature trigger and Super feature won from the Super Spicy wheel, a single or combination of **Super Extra Spins / Super Extra Pays / Super Extra Rows** is awarded.
- At the start of any Super feature, any [鑽] in the triggering spin will appear as [X]，which converts to [辣椒]。
- Any Super feature is played on **3×5 reel matrix**. Bonus reels are in play. **3 bonus spins are awarded initially.**（其餘同基本特色：填滿判定、reset 3、升級、結束派發）

## help-13 — SUPER EXTRA SPINS ／ SUPER EXTRA PAYS ／ SUPER EXTRA ROWS
- **SUPER EXTRA SPINS**：4 bonus spins instead of 3；additional lands reset remaining to 4；filled → 4 upgrades instead of 3。
- **SUPER EXTRA PAYS**：at start any [鑽] appears as [X] → converts to [辣椒]；feature 中 [辣椒] 可出現於任意位置；填滿後依升級規則升級。
- **SUPER EXTRA ROWS**：3 additional rows added，feature played on **6×5 reel matrix** when Super Extra rows is active。
