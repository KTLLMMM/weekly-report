## 02 PAYS
- WILD is WILD for [交叉魔杖+星星], [藍色錐帽], [魔藥瓶], A, K, Q, and J. **WILD only appears on reels 2, 3, and 4.**
- 交叉魔杖+星星（M1）：5=100, 4=50, 3=25, 2=10
- 藍色錐帽 / 魔藥瓶（Of a Kind）：5=50, 4=25, 3=10
- A K Q J（Of a Kind）：5=20, 4=10, 3=5
- Only highest winner paid per winning combination. All Reel Ways pays are multiplied by the bet multiplier. All Reel Ways must occur on adjacent reels, beginning with the leftmost reel. All pays in credits.

## 03 COIN VALUES
- Each Coin（星幣）displays a credit prize of **25-5000x bet multiplier** or **JACKPOT**.
- During the base game, Coin may also display（Feature Coin，圓形角色頭像）：Dusty（獨角獸/粉）、Baby Magic（綠錐）、Cash Wizard（藍）及其組合。
- The distribution of prizes displayed changes with bet option.

## 04 MAGIC FALLS FEATURE
- If every position on a reel is filled with Coins, then that reel awards the Magic Falls Feature.
- The Magic Falls Feature awards the prizes displayed on each of the corresponding reel's Coins.
- Coin displaying JACKPOT awards **MEGA, MAJOR, MINOR, or MINI**.
- If a Coin lands on a reel during a base game spin, then all Coins on that reel are **held for the next 3 wagers** at the same bet and denomination. The number of wagers that a reel's Coins will remain held is shown at the bottom of that reel during the base game spin.
- After a reel awards the Magic Falls Feature, the Coins currently on that reel are no longer held.
- Symbols that land behind Coins are not evaluated.

## 05 FREE GAMES FEATURE（觸發表 1/4）
- Feature Coins：Dusty（獨角獸）、Baby Magic（綠錐）、Cash Wizard（藍）及兩兩、三合一組合共 7 種。
- During the base game, Dusty Feature / Baby Magic Feature / Cash Wizard Feature / Dusty Baby Magic Feature / Dusty Cash Wizard Feature / Baby Magic Cash Wizard Feature / Dusty Baby Magic Cash Wizard Feature 可隨機觸發。
- Otherwise, during the base game, if every position on one or more reels is filled with Coins, and at least one of them displays a Feature Coin, then a Feature will be awarded based on the table.
- 表：Dusty Feature = only Dusty(獨角獸) coin on one or more reels；Baby Magic Feature = only Baby Magic(綠錐) coin on one or more reels.

## 06 FREE GAMES FEATURE（觸發表 2/4）

- Dusty Baby Magic Feature = Dusty + Baby Magic on reels，或 Dusty+Baby Magic 合體 coin。

## 07 FREE GAMES FEATURE（觸發表 3/4）

## 08 FREE GAMES FEATURE（觸發表 4/4 + 免費遊戲規則）
- Dusty Baby Magic Cash Wizard Feature（續）：多種合體 coin 組合。
- **8 free games are awarded.** Any Coin that lands during the free games will be held.
- Before each free game reel spin initiates, a number of positions may be replaced with Baby Magic(綠錐) or Cash Wizard(藍).

## 09 FREE GAMES FEATURE（三特色作用）
- If **Dusty（獨角獸）** appears, it may add **2-11 Coins** randomly on non-held positions.
- If **Baby Magic（綠錐）** appears, for each Coin, up to **5 credit prizes of 25-500x bet multiplier** are added to existing Coins on screen. Baby Magic cannot add prizes to Coins displaying JACKPOT.
- If **Cash Wizard（藍）** appears in a free game, then the prize displayed on each Coin is awarded. Additionally, JACKPOT will award MEGA, MAJOR, MINOR, or MINI for that free game.
- During a free game, if Magic Falls Feature is triggered, then **2 extra free games** are also awarded.

## 10 FREE GAMES FEATURE（特色 coin 可出現條件 + 結算順序）
- Dusty(獨角獸) appears only during Dusty / Dusty Baby Magic / Dusty Cash Wizard / Dusty Baby Magic Cash Wizard Feature.

- Cash Wizard(藍) appears only during Cash Wizard / Dusty Cash Wizard / Baby Magic Cash Wizard / Dusty Baby Magic Cash Wizard Feature.

## 11 FREE GAMES FEATURE（結算）
- At the conclusion of the free games, the prize displayed on each Coin is awarded.
- Additionally, JACKPOT will award MEGA, MAJOR, MINOR, or MINI.
- Upon exiting the Free Games Feature, the base game returns to the state that it was in prior to entering the Free Games Feature.
- An alternate set of reels is used during the Free Games Feature. All symbols other than Coin, Dusty, Baby Magic, and Cash Wizard are grayed out and not evaluated.

## 12 REEL WAYS™
- Total bet is 75 credits multiplied by the bet multiplier.
- Winning combinations may contain only one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. Like symbols, or their substitute, on a single reel are not part of the same winning combination but may create separate winning combinations.
- If a MEGA or MAJOR is awarded, then the value displayed in the corresponding meter is awarded and reset before any additional prizes are awarded.（→ MEGA / MAJOR 為累積彩金 meter）
- The amount wagered times the odds of winning the MEGA and MAJOR meters respectively are equivalent for all available denominations. Credit prizes are awarded as shown. The distribution of prizes awarded by JACKPOT changes with bet option.
