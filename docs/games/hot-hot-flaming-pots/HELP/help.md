## P5 — HOT FEATURE（help-05）

## P7 — REEL WAYS（彩金數值，help-07）

Total bet is **88 credits** multiplied by the bet multiplier.

Winning combinations may contain only one symbol from each adjacent reel, beginning with the leftmost reel. Like symbols, or their substitute, on a single reel are not part of the same winning combination but may create separate winning combinations.

- **MINOR** meter = 1880 credits × bet multiplier（固定彩金）
- **MINI** meter = 880 credits × bet multiplier（固定彩金）
- **GRAND** / **MAJOR** = 累積彩金（meter 顯示）

If GRAND or MAJOR is awarded, the value displayed on the corresponding meter is awarded and then the meter will reset before any additional prizes are awarded. If a multiplier is applied to MAJOR, then that prize will be awarded a number of times equal to the multiplier. The amount wagered times the odds of winning the GRAND or MAJOR meters respectively are equivalent for all available denominations.

The appearance of pots and fire over time is for presentation only. During the base game, the manner in which a number of positions are replaced with [HOT] is for presentation purposes only.
