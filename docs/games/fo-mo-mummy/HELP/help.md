## help-02 — JACKPOTS（彩金）
- **Grand, Major and Maxi are progressives.**（累積彩金）
- **Minor and Mini are fixed prizes.**（固定彩金）
- Jackpot meter amount is reset each time it has been paid, before any other Jackpots are paid.
- Once the Jackpot ceiling amount has been reached, further contributions are added to subsequent Jackpot progressives.
- Grand, Major, Maxi, Minor and Mini are not multiplied by the bet multiplier.
- An increase in wager increases the odds of winning progressives proportionally.
- The odds of Minor and Mini change with the bet multiplier.
- The odds of Minor and Mini change with different line options.
- The odds of jackpots change from bought games to Mo' features.

## help-03 — MO' FEATURES（頁1：觸發）
四色寶石各對應一種特色。有兩種觸發路徑：
- [Mr. Mummy] or [Pharaoh] with one or more **pink gem** → awards **Mo' Boosts** feature.
- [Mr. Mummy] or [Pharaoh] with one or more **green gem** → awards **Mo' Spins** feature.
- [Mr. Mummy] or [Pharaoh] with one or more **red gem** → awards **Mo' Prizes** feature.
- [Mr. Mummy] or [Pharaoh] with one or more **blue gem** → awards **Mo' Symbols** feature.
- One or more **pink gem** in a spin without [Mr. Mummy/Pharaoh] → may randomly award Mo' Boosts feature.
- One or more **green gem** in a spin without [scatter] → may randomly award Mo' Spins feature.
- One or more **red gem** in a spin without [scatter] → may randomly award Mo' Prizes feature.
- One or more **blue gem** in a spin without [scatter] → may randomly award Mo' Symbols feature.

## help-04 — MO' FEATURES（續：玩法框架 / 5×5）
- At the start of any Mo' feature, all [pink/green/red/blue gem or multi-colour gem] in the triggering spin convert into [diamond] with same prize displayed on them and copied to the corresponding positions.
- Feature is played on **5×5 reel matrix. All positions spin individually. 5 spins are awarded initially.** Bonus reels are in play.
- When any Mo' feature is triggered with [Mr. Mummy] or [Pharaoh]… will appear during the feature respectively.
- When any Mo' feature is triggered with [Mr. Mummy], at the start of the feature, [it] may randomly be upgraded to [Pharaoh].
- When any Mo' feature is triggered randomly, either [Mr. Mummy] or [Pharaoh] may appear during the feature.
- Initially **2×2** [Mr. Mummy] or [Pharaoh] appears and moves to all positions containing [diamond].
- After each feature spin, prizes displayed on the [diamond] and [ankh] which land within the [Mr. Mummy] or [Pharaoh] zone collected before the [collector] moves to positions containing the [diamond].
- Prizes displayed on each [diamond] are also collected when [Mr. Mummy] or [Pharaoh] move to their respective positions and collects them.
- Any [pink/green/red/blue] or [multi-colour gem] that appears during the feature converts to [diamond] and collected by [Mr. Mummy] or [Pharaoh].
- 1 additional spin is awarded if any inactive feature is activated.

## help-05 — MO' FEATURES（續：多特色併行 + Grand）
- During any single or combined Mo' features, remaining additional features can also be activated.
- [pink] [green] [red] or [blue] may appear and activate corresponding inactive features.
- [multi-colour gem] may appear and activate any one or more inactive features.
- If 1, 2 or 3 features are active, [gems] corresponding to inactive features along with [multi-colour], [diamond] and [ankh] may appear. If all 4 features are active, [diamond] or [ankh] may appear.
- Each [gem/diamond/ankh] has an associated prize displayed on it.
- Possible [pink/green/red or multi-colour] prizes during bought games: (0.4–100) × Total Bet, Grand, Major, Maxi, Minor or Mini.
- Possible [gems] and [ankh] prizes during Mo' features: (0.4–100) × Total Bet, Minor or Mini.
- The odds of credit prizes change with the bet multiplier and denomination.
- The odds of credit prizes change from bought games to Mo' features.
- **At most one Grand may appear** on [pink/green/red or multi-colour] in a bought game spin.

**MO' BOOSTS FEATURE**

**MO' SPINS FEATURE**
- When Mo' Spins is active, +1 Spin may also appear above [pink] [red] [blue] [multi-colour] [diamond] and [ankh].
- One additional feature spin is awarded when [diamond] or [ankh] with +1 Spin is collected.

## help-07 — MO' SYMBOLS FEATURE / MO' PRIZES FEATURE
**MO' SYMBOLS FEATURE**
- When Mo' Symbols is active, randomly [pink] [green] [red] [multi-colour] [diamond] or [ankh] may appear.

**MO' PRIZES FEATURE**
