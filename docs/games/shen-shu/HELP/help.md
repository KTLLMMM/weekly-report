## P1 — 投注／信用畫面（非規則）
Service ｜ Cash out ｜ 1¢ 2¢ 5¢ 10¢ ｜ 框值：80 / 160 / 240 / 320 / 400 / 600 CREDITS ｜ SHEN SHU ｜ "Malfunction voids all pays and plays."

## P2 — FEATURING（總覽）
- **INSTANT WIN ROW** — IN REGULAR GAME INSTANTLY AWARDS A CREDIT VALUE OR ANY FEATURE!
- **FREE GAMES** — WIN 10 FREE GAMES WITH 1 TO 3 FEATURES!
  - **ADD ALL FEATURE** — PRIZES ARE ADDING UP AND ADDING UP EVEN MORE!
  - **WHEEL POT FEATURE** — WIN 1 OR MORE JACKPOTS!
  - **MULTIPLIER FEATURE** — MULTIPLY A REEL'S INSTANT WINS BY UP TO 10X!
- **EXTRA ROW** — EXPANDS YOUR REELS FOR MORE CHANCES TO WIN!

## P4 — INSTANT WIN ROW
IN REGULAR GAME, AN INSTANT WIN ROW AT THE TOP OF THE REELS INCLUDES AN INSTANT WIN BOX FOR EACH REEL, WITH A POTENTIAL INSTANT WIN THAT CHANGES AT EVERY GAME:
- A CREDIT VALUE RANGING FROM 10 TO 750 CREDITS X THE BET MULTIPLIER, OR
- 1 OF THE 3 FREE GAME FEATURES (DETAILS IN THE NEXT PAGES).

WHEN [INSTANT WIN] LANDS ON A REEL, THE INSTANT WIN DISPLAYED ON THE TOP OF THAT REEL (A CREDIT VALUE OR A FREE GAME FEATURE) IS INSTANTLY AWARDED.

## P5 — FREE GAMES（觸發）
**RANDOM TRIGGER** — FREE GAMES ARE PLAYED WITH 1 TO 3 SPECIAL FEATURES. EACH FEATURE HAS ITS OWN POT ON TOP OF REELS, RANDOMLY TRIGGERED WITH FILLER ANIMATIONS, SENT BY ITS OWN TRIGGERING SYMBOL, PRESENT ON ALL 5 REELS.（ADD ALL FEATURE / WHEEL POT FEATURE / MULTIPLIER FEATURE … FILLS）

**AUTOMATIC TRIGGER** — WITH THE FEATURE IN THE INSTANT WIN BOX AND [INSTANT WIN] ON THE REEL BELOW, THE FEATURE IS AUTOMATICALLY TRIGGERED.

（註腳）THE LEVEL OF THE FEATURE POTS HAS NO EFFECT ON TRIGGERING FREE GAMES. AFTER A FREE GAMES SESSION, THE LEVEL OF THE TRIGGERED POT(S) IS RESET FOR ALL DENOMINATIONS.

## P6 — FREE GAMES PLAY
10 FREE GAMES ARE AWARDED AT THE TRIGGERING OF 1 TO 3 FEATURES (DETAILS IN NEXT PAGES). WHEN THE FREE GAMES START, EACH REEL'S INSTANT WIN BOX DISPLAYS A BASE VALUE OF 5 CREDITS TIMES THE BET MULTIPLIER. COINS WITH A CREDIT VALUE OR AN ACTIVATED FEATURE MAY LAND ON ANY OF THE 5 REELS DURING FREE GAMES. ALL CREDIT VALUES OR FEATURE WINS OBTAINED DURING A FREE GAMES SESSION ARE APPLIED TO THE INSTANT WIN BOX ABOVE THE REEL ON WHICH THE COIN LANDED. AT THE END OF THE FREE GAMES, THE 5 ACCUMULATED CREDIT VALUES ABOVE THE REELS ARE AWARDED. CREDIT VALUES [coin] RANGE FROM 5 TO 75 CREDITS X THE BET MULTIPLIER.

**EXTRA FREE GAMES** — DURING FREE GAMES, IT IS POSSIBLE TO INSTANTLY TRIGGER AN UNTRIGGERED FEATURE WHEN ITS CORRESPONDING SYMBOL LANDS ON ANY OF THE 5 REELS. 5 MORE FREE GAMES ARE AWARDED, WITH NEWLY AND PREVIOUSLY TRIGGERED FEATURES ACTIVE UNTIL THE END OF THE FREE GAMES SESSION.

## P7 — ADD ALL FEATURE ／ MULTIPLIER FEATURE
**ADD ALL FEATURE** — WHEN AN [ADD ALL] LANDS ON A REEL, A CREDIT VALUE EQUAL TO THE SUM OF THE VALUES IN THE 4 OTHER REEL INSTANT WIN BOXES IS DISPLAYED ON THE SYMBOL. THIS VALUE IS THEN ADDED TO THE CORRESPONDING REEL'S INSTANT WIN BOX. IF MORE THAN 1 FEATURE IS PLAYED DURING FREE GAMES, THE ADD ALL WINS ARE ALWAYS ACCUMULATED IN THE INSTANT WIN BOXES AT THE TOP OF THE REELS AFTER THE OTHER FEATURE WINS. WHEN MORE THAN 1 [ADD ALL] LANDS IN THE SAME GAME, EACH [ADD ALL] IS APPLIED ONE AT A TIME FROM THE LEFTMOST REEL TO THE RIGHT.

**MULTIPLIER FEATURE** — WHEN THE MULTIPLIER FEATURE IS ACTIVE, A RED COIN MAY LAND ON ANY OF THE 5 REELS. SPIN AND UNVEIL A MULTIPLIER 2X 3X 4X 5X OR 10X, WHICH WILL BE APPLIED TO THE CREDIT VALUE IN THE INSTANT WIN BOX OF THE CORRESPONDING REEL!

## P8 — WHEEL POT FEATURE
WHEN [WHEEL POT] LANDS ON A REEL, A VERTICAL WHEEL APPEARS ON TOP OF THE CORRESPONDING REEL, AND A SPIN IS AWARDED! THE FOLLOWING JACKPOTS CAN BE WON IN THE WHEEL POT FEATURE: GRAND, MAXI, MINOR, MINI. IF THE WHEEL STOPS ON "MINOR + NUDGE" OR "MINI + NUDGE", IT NUDGES UP OR DOWN FROM A RANDOM NUMBER OF POSITIONS TO AWARD 1 MORE JACKPOT UNTIL IT STOPS ON A SECTION WITHOUT "+ NUDGE". UP TO 6 JACKPOTS CAN BE AWARDED! THE CREDIT VALUES OF THE JACKPOTS ARE ADDED TO THE CORRESPONDING INSTANT WIN BOX, EXCEPT FOR THE GRAND AND MAXI JACKPOTS, THAT ARE SENT DIRECTLY IN THE WINS BOX AT THE BOTTOM OF THE REELS. EACH FREE GAMES FEATURE AND ANY COMBO OF FREE GAMES FEATURES ARE PLAYED WITH THEIR OWN SET OF REELS.

## P9 — JACKPOTS
THERE ARE 4 JACKPOTS IN THIS GAME, AVAILABLE AT ANY BET, EXCLUSIVELY IN FREE GAMES WITH THE WHEEL POT FEATURE:
- **FIXED JACKPOTS（固定彩金）**：MINI = 125 CREDITS X BET MULTIPLIER ｜ MINOR = 375 CREDITS X BET MULTIPLIER
- **PROGRESSIVE JACKPOTS（累積彩金）**：MAXI = 50,000 CREDITS AT ANY BET ｜ GRAND = 500,000 CREDITS AT ANY BET

ALL JACKPOTS VALUES ARE DISPLAYED IN MONEY AT THE TOP OF THE REELS AND ARE MULTIPLIED BY THE DENOMINATION VALUE. WHEN A PROGRESSIVE JACKPOT IS WON, IT IS RESET TO ITS BASE VALUE FOR THE CURRENT DENOMINATION, FOR ALL BET LEVELS.

## P10 — EXTRA ROW
DURING FREE GAMES, [EXTRA ROW] MAY LAND ON ANY REEL, EXPANDING YOUR REELS BY ONE ROW. UP TO 3 EXTRA ROWS MAY BE AWARDED DURING A FREE GAMES SESSION.

## P11 — TRIPLE BONUS METER
A TRIPLE BONUS METER MIGHT APPEAR ON TOP OF REELS TO INDICATE THAT FREE GAMES WITH ALL 3 FEATURES HAVE NOT BEEN TRIGGERED FOR A LONG TIME. THE METER IS RESET AND DISAPPEARS AT THE START OF THE FREE GAMES WITH THE 3 FEATURES. FREE GAMES FEATURES, SINGLE OR COMBINED, ARE PLAYED WITH THEIR OWN SETS OF REELS. ALL FREE GAMES WINS ARE ADDED TO OTHER WINS.
